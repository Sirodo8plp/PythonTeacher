Welcome to the offical user manual of PythonTeacher.

In this section you will learn how to use our, though complex, very beneficial teaching application.

First of all, nowadays you can't really do anything without an account. So first things first, register an account and start learning Python today. Or you sign in in immediately. For your account you only need a username and a password.

Each section, except for the first one which is considered an introduction, has an exercise at the end of the course in order to test your reading / understanding capabilities. You can complete these exercises by writing and submitting your answers. Your answers are recorded and stored in our database, so that you can view your progress in your profile page. Furthermore, the exercises determine the order of the exercises in practice tests.

You can traverse through sections in any order that you want. Once you complete the exercise in the respective section, though, you want be able to do it again in the section. However, you can encounter the same exercise in a practice test.

Your profile shows two graphs: The first graph shows the progress of your practice tests while the second one shows the progress of the exercises that you completed in a section.
